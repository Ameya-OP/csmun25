Description

DISEC, or the Disarmament and International Security Committee, is the First Committee of the United Nations General Assembly. It focuses on global security issues, particularly disarmament, arms control, and the prevention of weapons of mass destruction. DISEC addresses a wide range of topics, including nuclear disarmament, conventional arms control, regional conflicts, terrorism, and emerging threats like cybersecurity.
