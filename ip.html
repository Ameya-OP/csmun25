International Press (IP)

Agenda

Covering committee proceedings, conducting interviews, photography, and publication of daily newsletters.

Description

The International Press is the backbone of any MUN, delivering timely updates, in-depth analysis, and creative content. Delegates in IP take on the roles of journalists, photographers, and designers to cover debates, interview delegates, and produce media that brings the conference to life. It fosters creativity, critical thinking, and communication skills.
