Secretary General

s the Secretary-General of CSMUN 3.0, it is my distinct honour to welcome you to this landmark edition of our conference. Over the years, my journey through Model United Nations—as a delegate, chair, and now organiser—has been shaped by the belief that meaningful dialogue can drive real-world change. I am a passionate student of diplomacy, debate, and design, and I bring to this role not only my experience in MUNs, but also a deep commitment to creating spaces where diverse perspectives are heard and respected. CSMUN 3.0 reflects that vision. This conference has been carefully curated to balance intellectual challenge with inclusivity, offering each participant the opportunity to think critically, speak confidently, and lead empathetically. Behind the scenes, CSMUN 3.0 is the result of tireless planning, countless hours of collaboration, and an unshakable belief in the power of student-led initiatives. Our secretariat is a vibrant team of thinkers, creators, and problem-solvers who have worked with dedication to bring this conference to life. Outside of MUNs, my interests in programming, chess, and design have taught me the importance of strategy, creativity, and attention to detail—principles that have shaped the way we’ve built this year’s edition. Whether you’re returning to committee or experiencing your first conference, I encourage you to immerse yourself fully. Ask bold questions, build meaningful connections, and remember that your voice holds weight. On behalf of the entire Secretariat, I warmly welcome you to CSMUN 3.0—and I look forward to witnessing the impact you create.

Director General

It is a distinct honour to serve as the inaugural Director General of Interschool Cornerstone School MUN. I still remember walking into my first MUN session with hesitation—unsure of the procedures, uncertain of my own voice, and reluctant to even raise my placard. But somewhere in the midst of debate and late-night research, I discovered a space where diplomacy felt real and ideas sparked meaningful dialogue. With five conferences behind me, each has shaped my growth as a leader and deepened my belief in the power of youth-driven change. As I step into this role, my vision for CSMUN is rooted in three guiding values: authenticity, curiosity, and courage. I envision a conference where delegates don’t simply participate—they connect deeply, question critically, and lead boldly. Together with an exceptional Secretary General and a dedicated secretariat, we are committed to creating an experience that is not only intellectually enriching, but truly transformative.This first edition of Interschool CSMUN isn’t just the start of something official—it’s the start of something exciting. A space where bold ideas, sharp comebacks, and real conversations come together. We’re here to debate, to learn, to lead—and maybe even have a little fun along the way. Let’s make it count.
