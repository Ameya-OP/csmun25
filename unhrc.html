United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC)

Agenda

Addressing systemic discrimination and the protection of minority rights in conflict zones.

Description

The United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC) works to promote and protect human rights globally. It investigates human rights violations and offers a platform for nations to discuss justice, equality, and freedoms. The council also advises on human rights policy, encourages international cooperation, and holds nations accountable.
