United Nations Commission on Science and Technology for Development (UNCSTD)

Agenda

Deliberating on state surveillance and its implications on the right to privacy and freedom of expression.

Description

The United Nations Commission on Science and Technology for Development (UNCSTD) is a functional commission of the Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC). It serves as a focal point for the United Nations system in providing expert policy advice on all science and technology-related issues, especially those relevant to development.
